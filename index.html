For all of our programs in Bangladesh, we will locate villages and towns in need via our Board members and volunteers living in the areas. We will approach these communities and ask if they would be interested in working with us. We will then explain our programs and allow them to select what their community most needs. The corporation’s activities will focus on the teachings of our Lord, Jesus Christ, to feed the needy children of Africa regardless of their country of origin, and to provide shelter and food for the homeless children based on the Christian teaching. We will maintain and establish a safe environment for needy children, and provide ongoing basic healthcare needs including clothing and food. We will work with local health clinics and hospitals in Nigeria, the Ivory Coast, Ghana, Kenya, and the Benin Republic to help distribute vaccinations.
